## 1. Strategy: pillar + cluster (topic clusters) model

Each **pillar** is a broad, high-value guide that targets a head term and links out to many **cluster** articles (specific, long-tail). Cluster articles link back to the pillar and across to siblings, and **every article links to relevant money pages** (collections/PDPs). This concentrates authority and passes equity to commercial pages.

| # | Pillar | Pillar page targets | Money pages it feeds |
|---|--------|---------------------|----------------------|
| P1 | **Protein** | "protein powder Ireland", "whey protein" | `/collections/protein` |
| P2 | **Creatine** | "creatine Ireland", "creatine monohydrate" | `/collections/creatine` |
| P3 | **Pre-workout & energy** | "pre workout Ireland" | `/collections/pre-workout` |
| P4 | **Recovery & hydration** | "muscle recovery supplements" | `/collections/recovery` |
| P5 | **Weight management** | "fat loss supplements / weight management" | `/collections/weight-management` |
| P6 | **Women's sports nutrition** | "protein powder for women Ireland" | `/collections/protein`, bundles |
| P7 | **Beginners / fundamentals** | "supplements for beginners", "how to start" | bundles, bestsellers |
| P8 | **Buying guides & Irish/local** | "best protein powder Ireland", comparisons | collections, PDPs |

**Funnel stages:** **TOFU** (awareness/informational) → **MOFU** (consideration/how-to) → **BOFU** (commercial/comparison, closest to purchase).
**Priority key:** 🟢 = quick win (low difficulty, build first) · 🟡 = medium · 🔴 = competitive/long-game (build authority before attempting).

## 3. Prioritisation & sequencing

1. **Months 1–3 — quick wins (🟢):** publish a cluster of low-difficulty articles around each money page so collections aren't orphaned. Aim for breadth that signals topical coverage.
2. **Months 2–4 — pillars (🔴 hubs):** publish the 8 pillar pages once several clusters exist to link into them.
3. **Months 4+ — competitive BOFU (🔴):** attempt "best protein powder Ireland" type terms once you have authority + links.
4. **Continuous:** refresh/update cornerstone pieces; expand clusters based on Search Console query data (your real ranking opportunities).

**Cadence on lean budget:** **4–6 articles/month** (your time or a freelance writer at €30–80/article). ~10 months to complete all 50; quick wins start ranking along the way.

## 6. On-page SEO checklist (per article)

- [ ] One primary keyword; no cannibalisation with existing pages.
- [ ] Keyword in title, H1, URL, first 100 words, naturally throughout.
- [ ] Unique meta title & description.
- [ ] Logical H2/H3 structure; answers the query completely.
- [ ] FAQ section → `FAQPage` schema; `Article` schema with author/reviewer.
- [ ] ≥2 internal links to money pages + sibling articles; links up to pillar.
- [ ] Compressed images with descriptive alt text.
- [ ] Author bio + reviewer (E-E-A-T); citations to reputable sources.
- [ ] **Compliance pass**: only authorised health/nutrition claims; disclaimer present (see [06](./06-REGULATORY-COMPLIANCE.md)).
- [ ] Compelling, scannable intro + key-takeaways box for featured-snippet capture.

## 7. Compliance note for content (critical)

Articles are health content for a commercial site → **YMYL + advertising**. You must **not** make unauthorised health/disease claims (e.g. "cures", "treats", "prevents illness", "guarantees fat loss"). Use only **EU-authorised** nutrition/health claims, attribute physiological statements to authorised wording, cite sources, and include the standard disclaimer ("not intended to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease; supplements support a balanced diet, not replace it"). Full rules in [06](./06-REGULATORY-COMPLIANCE.md). When in doubt, soften to education, not promise.
