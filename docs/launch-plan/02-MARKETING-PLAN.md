# 02 — Marketing Plan (Organic-First, Lean)

> Budget: **< €1,000/month.** Therefore: owned + earned channels first, paid as a small surgical supplement. Retention is the growth engine.

## 1. Positioning

**Positioning statement (template to finalise with brand work):**
> For Irish athletes and gym-goers who are tired of slow, customs-hit deliveries from UK supplement giants, **[Brand]** is the Irish-made, Informed-Sport-tested sports nutrition brand that delivers next-day across Ireland at honest, transparent prices — so you can trust exactly what's in the tub and when it'll arrive.

**Brand pillars** (everything ladders up to these):
1. **Trust / transparency** — Informed Sport tested, full supplement facts, COAs published.
2. **Irish & fast** — local brand, next-day delivery, no customs friction (post-Brexit wedge).
3. **Honest value** — fair price-per-serving, bundles, subscribe & save (not "cheapest at all costs").
4. **Performance for everyone** — beginners to athletes, men and women, framed inclusively.

**Why not "cheapest":** you can't out-price scaled UK players on a lean budget (see [05](./05-COMPETITIVE-PRICING-RESEARCH.md)). Lead with *trust + speed + value*; undercut tactically on a few hero SKUs.

## 4. Phased launch sequence

### Phase A — Pre-launch (build the list before you have a store)
- Landing page with email capture + "founding customer" incentive (e.g. 20% off first order + free shaker).
- Tease the brand on social; build in public; seed micro-influencers with "coming soon".
- Goal: **500–1,000 emails before launch day.**

### Phase B — Launch
- Launch offer to the pre-list first (exclusivity), then public.
- Influencer posts go live in a cluster (manufactured momentum).
- Local PR push (Irish-owned story).
- First reviews are gold — incentivise early buyers to review (compliantly).

### Phase C — Post-launch (retention + content compounding)
- Turn on full lifecycle email/SMS flows.
- Publish content cadence (doc 04) and build internal links.
- Launch Subscribe & Save + loyalty/referral.
- Begin link-building outreach (doc 03).

See timeline in [07](./07-LAUNCH-ROADMAP.md).

## 5. Email/SMS lifecycle flows (set up before launch)

| Flow | Trigger | Goal | Key messages |
|------|---------|------|--------------|
| **Welcome** | Signup | First purchase | Brand story, Informed Sport, incentive, bestsellers |
| **Abandoned cart** | Cart not purchased | Recover | Reminder → social proof → (last) incentive |
| **Abandoned browse** | Viewed, no add | Nudge | Helpful, not pushy |
| **Post-purchase** | Order placed | Onboard + reduce anxiety | Shipping, how-to-use, "what to expect" |
| **Review request** | X days after delivery | Social proof | Easy review ask + photo prompt (compliant) |
| **Replenishment** | ~Days-supply elapsed | Repeat purchase | "Running low? Subscribe & save" |
| **Subscription lifecycle** | Sub active | Reduce churn | Upcoming charge, easy skip/swap, rewards |
| **Win-back** | Lapsed 60–90d | Reactivate | "We miss you" + incentive |
| **VIP / loyalty** | High LTV | Reward + advocacy | Early access, referral push |

> Newsletter cadence: weekly-ish — mix education (repurpose doc-04 articles), product, and Irish community content. Always include compliant claims only.

## 6. Retention & LTV — the lean-budget engine

Because paid acquisition is constrained, **profit comes from repeat purchase**:
1. **Subscribe & Save** (10–15% off + free shipping) — protein/creatine are perfect for replenishment. Target 15–25% of orders.
2. **Loyalty program** — points on spend/reviews/referrals → redeemable. Encourages repeat + UGC.
3. **Referral** — give-€X-get-€X. Your happy customers become your cheapest acquisition channel.
4. **Bundles & stacks** — raise AOV and lock in multi-product habits.
5. **Best-in-class CX** — fast delivery, easy returns, responsive support → reviews → trust → SEO + conversion flywheel.

## 7. Budget allocation (< €1,000/month)

| Item | Monthly | Notes |
|------|---------|-------|
| Email/SMS platform (Klaviyo/Omnisend) | €0–60 | Free tier early, scales with list |
| Reviews app (Judge.me/Trustpilot) | €0–50 | Free/low tiers exist |
| Content production (freelance writer/your time) | €150–350 | 4–6 articles/mo at quick-win keywords |
| Influencer seeding (product cost, not cash) | €50–150 | Gifted product COGS |
| Design/asset tools (Canva, etc.) | €0–30 | |
| Paid ads (capped, optional) | €100–300 | Brand defence + retargeting + one test campaign |
| SEO tooling | €0–100 | Search Console free; consider one paid tool (Ahrefs Lite/Mangools) when affordable |
| Contingency / sampling / PR | €50–150 | Local gym sampling, press outreach |
| **Total** | **~€400–900** | Leaves headroom; bias spend to content + retention |

**When budget grows (>€2–3k/mo):** scale paid (Google Shopping/PMax on best-sellers, Meta prospecting with UGC creative), increase content velocity, paid influencer partnerships, and possibly a PR retainer. The organic foundation makes every future paid euro cheaper.
