# 07 — Launch Roadmap

> Phased execution plan tying together all other documents. **Critical path: regulatory + supply must clear before marketing spend and selling.** Content + site build run in parallel because SEO takes months to mature.
> Related: [00 Overview](./00-OVERVIEW.md) · [06 Compliance](./06-REGULATORY-COMPLIANCE.md)

---

## 1. Phase overview

| Phase | Theme | Approx. duration | Gate to next phase |
|-------|-------|------------------|--------------------|
| **0** | Foundation: compliance, supply, brand | Months 0–3 | Products notified, labels signed off, stock inbound |
| **1** | Build: site + content engine | Months 1–4 (overlaps 0) | Store transaction-ready, first content live |
| **2** | Soft launch + list building | Months 3–5 | 500–1,000 email list, reviews seeded |
| **3** | Public launch | Month 5 | Launch executed, first sales & reviews |
| **4** | Scale & retention | Month 6+ | Repeat-rate & SEO compounding |

> Durations are indicative for a lean, small team — compliance and manufacturing lead times are the main variables.

---

## 2. Phase 0 — Foundation (critical path)

**Compliance & legal** ([06](./06-REGULATORY-COMPLIANCE.md))
- Engage food-law specialist, solicitor, accountant.
- Company (CRO) + business bank + VAT registration.
- FSAI notification per product; label sign-off; claims audit framework.

**Supply & quality**
- Select white-label manufacturer (EU/IE, GMP); negotiate MOQs/lead times/cost tiers.
- Arrange COAs + **Informed Sport** testing for hero SKUs.
- Confirm landed cost per SKU → feeds pricing ([05](./05-COMPETITIVE-PRICING-RESEARCH.md)).
- Set up 3PL/fulfilment + returns.

**Brand & economics**
- Brand identity (name, logo, pillars, tone) → feeds [01](./01-WEBSITE-DESIGN-PLAN.md)/[02](./02-MARKETING-PLAN.md).
- Build unit-economics model; set prices & price floors.
- Stand up competitor price-tracking sheet ([05](./05-COMPETITIVE-PRICING-RESEARCH.md)).

🚦 **Gate:** products legally sellable + stock arriving.

---

## 3. Phase 1 — Build (parallel with Phase 0)

**Site** ([01](./01-WEBSITE-DESIGN-PLAN.md))
- Confirm platform (Shopify vs headless Next.js this repo).
- Design system → core pages: home, 1 collection, 1 PDP template → roll out catalogue.
- Cart/checkout + payments (cards, Apple/Google Pay, Revolut Pay, PayPal).
- Reviews, subscriptions, email capture integrations.
- Legal pages, disclaimer, analytics (GA4 + **Search Console**), structured data.

**SEO + content** ([03](./03-SEO-PLAN.md), [04](./04-CONTENT-STRATEGY-50-ARTICLES.md)) — **start ASAP, it's slow to mature**
- Technical SEO baseline; sitemaps; schema; CWV in budget.
- Publish first **quick-win** articles (months 1–2 of editorial calendar) + collection intro copy.
- Begin internal-linking model; verify GBP.

🚦 **Gate:** store transaction-ready; analytics + first content live.

---

## 4. Phase 2 — Soft launch & list building ([02](./02-MARKETING-PLAN.md))

- Pre-launch landing page + email capture; founding-customer incentive.
- Seed micro-influencers/gyms with product (gifted); tease on social.
- Friends/family/early orders → **seed first reviews** (compliantly).
- Stand up email/SMS lifecycle flows so they're live at launch.
- Keep publishing content (4–6/mo).

🚦 **Gate:** 500–1,000 emails; flows live; initial reviews on hero SKUs.

---

## 5. Phase 3 — Public launch

- Launch offer to pre-list first (exclusivity), then public.
- Influencer posts go live in a cluster; local **PR push** (Irish-owned story → backlinks).
- Turn on Subscribe & Save, loyalty, referral.
- Optional capped paid: brand-term defence + retargeting.
- Monitor: orders, conversion rate, AOV, stock, CX/reviews.

🚦 **Gate:** launch executed; first sales + review velocity.

---

## 6. Phase 4 — Scale & retention (month 6+)

- **Retention engine** ([02 §6](./02-MARKETING-PLAN.md)): drive subscription %, repeat rate, referrals — the lean-budget profit source.
- **SEO compounding**: finish the 50 articles; build pillar pages; pursue BOFU/commercial terms; earn backlinks (digital PR, partnerships, HARO).
- **Pricing**: weekly competitor tracking; reprice hero SKUs; protect margin via bundles/subscriptions ([05](./05-COMPETITIVE-PRICING-RESEARCH.md)).
- **Reinvest profit** into more content and, as LTV proves out, scale paid (Google Shopping/PMax, Meta with UGC).
- Range expansion based on data (Search Console queries, best-sellers, customer requests).

## 7. KPI dashboard & review cadence

| Cadence | Metrics (from [00 §4](./00-OVERVIEW.md#4-kpi-framework--12-month-north-star-targets)) |
|---------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Weekly** | Sessions, top landing pages, conversion rate, orders, AOV, email-list growth, GSC position-11–20 opportunities, hero-SKU competitor prices |
| **Monthly** | Organic sessions & revenue, keyword rankings (IE), backlinks, gross margin by SKU, competitor price index, retention/subscription %, email % of revenue, CWV |
| **Quarterly** | Cohort LTV, content audit/refresh, competitor-set review, MOQ/cost renegotiation, range/pricing strategy |

## 8. Critical-path callout (read before spending a euro)

```
Compliance sign-off  ─┐
Stock inbound        ─┼──►  Can SELL  ──►  Public launch  ──►  Paid spend
Store transaction-   ─┘
ready
        (Content + SEO start in parallel from day 1 — they mature slowly)
```

**Do not** run ads or open sales before regulatory sign-off and labelled, tested stock are in hand. Everything else can overlap; compliance is the gate.
